### Section Headline
**Frequently Asked Questions**

### Q: What exactly is an AI fleet?
An AI fleet is a coordinated team of specialized AI agents that handle different business functions—customer service, data processing, sales support, operations, and more. Unlike single-point solutions, our fleets work together, share information, and provide comprehensive automation.

### Q: How is this different from chatbots or automation tools?
Chatbots handle simple Q&A. Automation tools handle single tasks. An AI fleet is a workforce—multiple agents handling complex, interconnected workflows with coordination, decision-making, and continuous learning.

### Q: Do you only work with Newark businesses?
We're based in Newark, CA and specialize in Newark, Fremont, and East Bay businesses. We understand local industries, regulations, and business cultures. For larger engagements, we serve the broader Bay Area.

### Q: How long does deployment take?
Standard deployment is 2 weeks from signed agreement to full operation. Complex enterprise deployments may take 3-4 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during your fleet audit.

### Q: Will this replace my employees?
No—it elevates them. Your team stops doing repetitive work and focuses on high-value activities: strategy, relationships, complex problem-solving. Most clients find retention improves because work becomes more engaging.

### Q: What systems do you integrate with?
We integrate with 100+ business systems including Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics, NetSuite, SAP, QuickBooks, Zendesk, Shopify, and custom ERPs. Don't see your system? We build custom integrations.

### Q: What if the AI makes mistakes?
During the first 30 days, we operate in "supervised mode" where you review agent actions before they're finalized. Once accuracy meets your standards, we switch to full automation with exception reporting. You maintain oversight and can adjust anytime.

### Q: Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We're SOC 2 Type II certified. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use enterprise-grade security. Your data is never used to train general models or shared with third parties.

### Q: What's the 90-day guarantee?
If your AI fleet doesn't deliver the projected ROI within 90 days, we'll refund 100% of your fees. We only succeed when you succeed.

### Q: How do I get started?
Book a free fleet audit. We'll analyze your operations, identify automation opportunities, and provide a detailed proposal with guaranteed ROI projections. No obligation, no pressure.
