**A:** The $2,500 setup covers your dedicated fleet design, system integration, agent training, and deployment. It's a one-time investment that typically pays for itself in the first week of operation.

### Q: What's included in "transactions"?
**A:** A transaction is any completed workflow—an email response, a data entry, a scheduled meeting, a processed order, etc. Most businesses never hit their limit. If you do, we simply adjust your plan.

### Q: Will the price increase?
**A:** Your rate is locked for the first 12 months. After that, we guarantee no more than 5% annual increases for existing customers. New customers may see higher rates as we add capabilities.

**A:** We're Newark-based. We serve Newark businesses. We want AI automation accessible to the small and mid-size companies that drive our local economy—not just Fortune 500s.

### Q: What if we don't see ROI?
**A:** Our 90-day guarantee means zero risk. If you don't achieve projected ROI within 90 days, you get a full refund. We've never had to honor it, but it's there for your peace of mind.

### Q: Are there hidden fees?
**A:** Absolutely not. Monthly fee + one-time setup. That's it. No integration fees. No support fees. No overage fees. No cancellation fees.

### Q: Can we start small and upgrade?
**A:** Yes—most clients do. Start with Starter Fleet, prove value, then upgrade to Professional as you expand automation.
