## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Visual Style:
- Professional, modern interface mockups
- Newark location B-roll for local credibility
- Fast transitions (no slow fades)
- Clean, corporate color palette (blues, grays)
- Actual screen recordings preferred over animations

### Audio:
- Professional voiceover (confident, trustworthy)
- Modern background music (corporate but not sterile)
- Sound effects for UI actions (subtle)

### Pacing:
- Quick cuts in first 30 seconds (hook)
- Slightly slower during demos (clarity)
- Build energy toward CTA
- Emphasize "Newark, CA" for local positioning
